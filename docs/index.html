Overview

Relevance higher is better

Known-item metrics, all on a 0–1 scale. Success@10: the answer appears in the top 10. MRR: how high the first correct answer ranks. Recall@10: share of a query’s correct answers reaching the top 10.

Latency, ms lower is better

Warm caches, fastest-of-5 per query. p50 is the typical feel; p99 is the tail you actually notice.

Indexing

By query category

Per-category score, engine × category. Darker is stronger (0–1 scale). Six categories, 25 queries each.

Significance paired MRR, 95% bootstrap CI

Each panel compares two engines on per-query MRR, overall and per category. The bar spans the 95% confidence interval of the mean difference; the dot is the point estimate. Positive favors the first engine. A bar that clears the zero line (marked ∗) is significant.
